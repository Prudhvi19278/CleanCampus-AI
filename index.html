Project Title

CleanCampus AI – Smart Campus Cleanliness Monitoring System

SDG Alignment

SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities
SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production

Problem Statement

How might we use AI to monitor cleanliness issues on university campuses and enable timely action to maintain a clean, healthy, and sustainable campus environment?

Target Users

  • University students
  • Campus housekeeping staff
  • Faculty and administrators
  • Educational institutions

AI Solution Overview

CleanCampus AI is a conceptual AI-based system that allows users to report cleanliness issues on campus. The system uses AI concepts to analyze reported issues, categorize waste types, assign priority levels, and support campus authorities in maintaining hygiene and sustainability.

Role of AI

  • Analyzes reported cleanliness issues
  • Categorizes waste such as food, plastic, and litter
  • Assigns priority based on severity
  • Provides insights for better cleanliness planning

Responsible AI Considerations

The system follows responsible AI principles by avoiding personal data collection, ensuring transparency, and using AI only to support positive environmental and social impact.

Expected Impact

This project helps improve campus hygiene, reduce improper waste disposal, increase awareness about cleanliness, and promote sustainable practices among students and staff.
